I am currently a final year undergraduate student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I am a member of IEEE honor class, an elite student program in the School of Electronics, Information and Electrical Engineering.

My current research interests lie in the field of three dimensional computer vision and its applications. Previously, I worked on person re-identification for one year.

I have the greatest fortune to work with Prof. Song Han from MIT and Profs. Cewu Lu and Hongtao Lu from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in the past two years. I am also grateful to be supervised by Dr. Ying Tai at Tencent Youtu Lab, where I worked as an intern researcher.

I will be applying for CS graduate programs in computer vision, machine learning and computer systems starting Fall 2020, take a look at my CV if you are interested.
